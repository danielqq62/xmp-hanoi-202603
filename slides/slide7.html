The Challenge: Manual Ops Limit Scale on TikTok

Growing on TikTok requires speed and volume, but traditional manual workflows create critical bottlenecks that slow down testing and optimization.

Manual Campaign Setup

Manually creating hundreds of ad groups for Smart+ testing takes hours. Repetitive clicking blocks rapid iteration and delays launch times.

Creative Chaos

Managing high volumes of video assets across local drives and folders makes it impossible to track version history or organize by concept.

Data in Different Places

UA and design teams use different tools. Designers can’t see performance like ROAS or retention, so it’s hard to know what works.

No Clear Rules

Without clear permissions and naming rules, ad accounts get messy, reports are inconsistent, and mistakes can happen.

The Outcome: Costly iteration cycles, delayed learnings, and missed Smart+ momentum
